Lazar van Berchem

I am currently a third-year undergraduate student in the CPES program Data Science, Arts and Cultures, jointly offered by the Lycée Louis-le-Grand and Université PSL. My studies cover both humanities — such as literature and art history — and sciences — such as mathematics and algorithmic. This interdisciplinary program allows me to apply quantitative tools to the analysis of cultural data, such as text analysis for example. You can find my curriculum vitæ at this link.

If you have any questions, don't hesitate to send me an email at: lazar.vanberchem@psl.eu.
